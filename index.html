Swipe Photo Cleaner is built as a commercial utility app. This service is provided by Guillermo A. Beuchat and is intended for use as is.

🛡️ Absolute Privacy Guarantee

Swipe Photo Cleaner is designed with a "Privacy First" architecture. The app operates with the following strict principles:

  • 100% Anonymous: No registration, sign-up, or user accounts are required. We do not know who you are.
  • 100% Offline: All media scanning, analysis, and file deletions are processed entirely local on your device. The app requests no internet permissions, meaning it works perfectly in Airplane Mode. No files ever leave your device.
  • 100% Ad-Free: There are no ads, trackers, analytics packages, or third-party telemetry libraries included.
  • Unlimited Access: No paywalls, usage limits, or hidden fees.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, device data, or media files. The storage access permissions requested on your device are utilized strictly by the app local user interface to display your photos, videos, and GIFs, and to delegate deletion requests to the Android System MediaStore.
